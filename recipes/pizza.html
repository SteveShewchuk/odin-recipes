Pizza Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for a fantastic homemade pizza. I'd tell you my life story like the other recipe sites but you really don't want to read that.

Ingrediants

  • 1 Can Tomato Pure
  • 1 Pizza Dough
  • 6 oz. Cheese
  • 2 Tbs. Olive Oil
  • 3 Tbs. Fresh Oregano

Steps:

  1. Step 1: Preheat the Oven to 400˚F
  2. Step 2: Put a cast item pan in the oven to heat up
  3. Step 3: Stretch the dough out on a cuttng board
  4. Step 4: Spread Tomato pure over the dough and add the cheese
  5. Step 5: Drizzle the olive oil over the pizza
  6. Step 6: Remove the cast iron pan from the oven and carefully slide the pizza from the cutting board onto the pan. Return the pan to the oven to 11 minutes.
  7. Step 7: Top with Oregano and salt
